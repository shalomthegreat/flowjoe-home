### The customer is...

> Someone building something complex over time.

That could be:

- an app
- an album
- a book
- a startup
- a research project
- a YouTube channel
- a game

The shared pain isn't:

> I need task management.

The shared pain is:

> My project is becoming larger than what I can comfortably hold in my head.

FlowJoe's job is to become the context layer and visual map that allows that project to continue growing without becoming overwhelming.

> Keep the project alive, understandable, and moving forward as it grows.

That's a very compelling promise because both the solo developer and the musician immediately recognize it as their problem.
